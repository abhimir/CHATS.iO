About Us (home)

CHATS.iO is a new social chat site created by @spadoll and @chrismatthieu. The goal of this site is to create space where conversations can happen dynamically and naturally without logins or registrations. We were aiming to create a cross between Twitter and IRC without setting up accounts or installing software. Chats can be public or private and can be linked to by including any topic/chatroom name in the URL like http://chats.io/party.

The technology is written in Node.JS and deployed on Nodester. Node.JS is the latest event-driven technology designed for real-time communications. CHATS.iO uses websockets to push messages to the users' browsers immediately as messages are sent.
